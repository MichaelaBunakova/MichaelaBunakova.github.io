SOCIOLOGY · SOCIAL EPIDEMIOLOGY · DEMOGRAPHY

Health, policy, and
social inequality
in Canada.

PhD Candidate in Sociology at McGill University (PhD5) and Wolfe Chair 2025/2026 Fellow. My research sits at the intersection of social epidemiology and policy, examining how educational and literacy spillovers from family social network structures affect health inequalities in Canada. Supervised by Professor Amélie Quesnel-Vallée and funded by the FRQSC Doctoral Research Grant.
